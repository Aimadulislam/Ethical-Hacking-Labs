# ⚖️ Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

## 1. Our Pledge
We, as members, contributors, and leaders of the **Secure Sandbox** community, pledge to make participation in our project and community a harassment-free and deeply educational experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, visible or invisible disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity, technical experience, or educational background.

We pledge to act and interact in ways that contribute to an open, welcoming, diverse, inclusive, and healthy community.

---

## 2. Our Standards

### 👍 Positive Behaviors
Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment include:
* Using welcoming, respectful, and inclusive language.
* Being respectful of differing viewpoints, methodologies, and technical experiences.
* Gracefully accepting and applying constructive feedback.
* Focusing on what is best for the educational integrity of the community.
* Showing empathy and professional courtesy toward other community members.

### 👎 Unacceptable Behaviors
Examples of unacceptable behavior include:
* The use of sexualized language or imagery, and unwelcome sexual attention or advances.
* Trolling, insulting, derogatory, or personal comments, and political or sectarian attacks.
* Public or private harassment of any kind.
* Publishing others' private information (such as physical or email addresses) without explicit, written permission.
* **Encouraging, planning, or executing unauthorized network intrusions, scanning of public assets, or malicious hacking operations.**
* Other conduct which could reasonably be considered inappropriate in a professional academic environment.

---

## 3. Enforcement Responsibilities
Community leaders are responsible for clarifying and enforcing our standards of acceptable behavior. They will take fair and timely corrective actions in response to any behavior that they deem inappropriate, threatening, or harmful.

Community leaders have the right and responsibility to edit, reject, or delete commits, pull requests, issues, or comments that do not align with this Code of Conduct, and to temporarily or permanently suspend any contributor for behaviors they deem inappropriate or harmful.

---

## 4. Scope
This Code of Conduct applies within all community spaces associated with the **Secure Sandbox** project. It also applies when an individual is officially representing the project or its community in public spaces (such as workshops, social media, or academic forums).

---

## 5. Reporting and Enforcement Guidelines
If you witness or experience behavior that violates this Code of Conduct, please report it immediately to the project maintainers. All complaints will be reviewed and investigated promptly, fairly, and with absolute confidentiality.

Upon receiving a report, project maintainers will:
1. **Investigate**: Gather facts, logs, and statements from all involved parties.
2. **Determine Action**: Decide if a violation occurred and choose the appropriate response (ranging from a private warning to permanent account suspension).
3. **Follow Up**: Notify the reporter of the decision and action taken, maintaining safety and privacy boundaries.
